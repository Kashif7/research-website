Our Research Areas

Vectorization

Conversion of textual context into a numeric representation that would act as the input for the neural network.

Attention

Focusing only on the facts necessary for answer generation and discarding unnecessary parts of the context.

Memory

Utilizing the memory optimally and reducing overhead.

Reasoning

Choosing the best path to arrive at the answer by evaluating the necessary facts and deducing an answer.
